My Family

My family is the greatest family ever. There are 7 of us, Hannah, Mitchell, Skylar, Mom, Dad, Punch, and Myself.

Hannah is probably my favorite owner. She was the one who gave me the name Oakley, and when I was a tiny little energetic puppy she would take me for 3 walks a day. That's so many walks. One day I helped her pack up the trunk of mom's car and move alllll the way to Orono. I did the math, it takes 3 hours to drive there, not fun. When Hannah is gone at school Skylar and I stay in her bed because we miss her so much. Sometimes Mitchell calls Hannah on facetime and I get excited because I hear her voice and think that she is home. Sadly she only comes home for a few days every couple of months now.

Mitchell is my buddy. He scratches my belly and gives me hugs when Punch tries to bite me. Punch was just getting used to being the only dog when I came around and basically ruined his life, so he tries to bite me a lot. Skylar is fun sometimes, but she kicks me in her sleep. Sometimes I'm pretty sure she gets me mistaken with a horse.
